Right of First Refusal

Subject to certain conditions, we granted US Tiger and EF Hutton, for a period of 24-months after the date of the consummation of our business combination, a right of first refusal to act as sole book runner, and/or sole placement agent, at US Tiger and EF Hutton’s sole discretion, for each and every future public and private equity and debt offering, including all equity linked financings for us or any of our successors or subsidiaries. In accordance with FINRA Rule 5110(g)(6)(A), such right of first refusal shall not have a duration of more than three years from the effective date of the registration statement of which this prospectus forms a part.
